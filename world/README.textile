h1. World

The world of Naruto takes place in a semi-modern time-period where war and political intrigue can happen at any moment.

h2. Layout

- Continent := a collection of nations surrounded by oceans.
- Nation := land which is goverened by fuedal lords. These lands contain many towns, cities and villages. The lord usually resides in the capital.
- "Hidden Villages":./hidden-village.textile := a hidden village, usually created/funded by their nation's lord that contains shinobi clans. The hidden villages are goverened by a Kage _(shadow)_, who assigns missions and operations for that are beneficial to their nation or allies.
- Villages/Towns := locations which are scattered around nations who do not have to be affiliated with the nation they reside in. They are usually run by a mayor, lord, or governor.
